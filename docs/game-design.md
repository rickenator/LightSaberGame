# Game Design

## Core loop

1. Enter an encounter.
2. Track and strike targets with blade actions.
3. Defend with timing-based block and parry windows.
4. Score points, avoid critical hits, and advance.

## Controls

- Touch controls for aiming and swing direction
- Tilt support for responsive motion accents
- Optional haptics for contact and warning events

## Blade mechanics

- Swing: primary attack arc
- Block: directional defense state
- Parry: tight-timing counter interaction
- Throw: cooldown-based ranged blade action

## Opponents

Progression path:

1. Training drone
2. Patrol sentry
3. Duelist opponent

## Levels and progression

- Encounters increase speed and pattern complexity
- Milestones unlock new challenge sets
- Score and consistency drive rank progression

## Scoring

- Hit accuracy bonuses
- Combo multipliers
- Defense timing bonuses for blocks/parries

## Fail states

- Health depletion
- Timeout on objective encounters
- Score floor miss on challenge modes

## Audio cues

- Distinct swing, impact, block, and warning cues
- Layered intensity as encounter pressure rises

## Haptics

- Light pulse for contact
- Medium pulse for blocked impact
- Strong pulse for critical damage
