# Assets Guidelines

## Texture formats

- Use PNG for texture assets
- Maximum texture size: 1024
- Prefer power-of-two dimensions where practical

## Audio formats

- Use OGG for compressed audio assets
- Keep one-shot effects short and normalized

## Licensing

Third-party assets must be compatible with Apache-2.0 distribution, including
compatible CC0 or CC-BY assets when attribution requirements are met.

Record all attributions in `docs/credits.md`.
